El Gato, Mi Gato 1989 Prototype

Developed byFelixNloom

EGMG 1989 takes place in an alternate reality where video games are illegal and drug use is legal, and even encouraged. In this game all you're trying to do is get your Game Station 9001 set up in time to play before you have to go back to work tomorrow. Maybe your live-in android roommate can give you some help.
