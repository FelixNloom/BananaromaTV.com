Ways to Contribute to BTV

You can help BananaROMa TV in various ways.

Basically Free Ways to Contribute via Twitch


1. Free Subscription with your Amazon Prime Membership

If you have an Amazon Prime membership you can subscribe to BananROMa TV for free on our live streaming channel here Twitch.tv/BananaROMa

What is required to contribute this way?

  • - An Amazon account with an active Prime membership *Amazon Prime is a paid service provided by Amazon.com
  • - An active Twitch account that is connected to said Amazon account *Twitch Accounts are free
  • - The will power to press a purple button that says "subscribe"

Why would I want to contribute this way?

Many people with Amazon Prime accounts are not aware that they have a free Twitch subscription included with the myriad things Prime members recieve. Every time you click that purple "Subscribe" button on our Twitch page we get $2.50. You can click the button once every month. It may seem small, but that money adds up, and it doesn't cost you anythin extra.

  • *Not sure how to link your Twitch account to Amazon Prime? Click here!

  • *Need to sign up for a FREE Twitch account? Click here to sign up for Twitch!
